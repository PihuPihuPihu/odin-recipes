Mango Lassi


Home
Ingredients
  • 1 cup nonfat plain yogurt
  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1/4 cup of mango pulp w juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground ginger

Steps

  1. Blend all the ingredients in a blender or mixer
  2. Now you delicious mango lassi is ready to serve. Serve with or without ice !
